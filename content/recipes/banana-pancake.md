tags: [kids, breakfast]

## Ingredients

- 240g plain white flour
- 270ml milk
- 2 ripe bananas, mashed
- 2 tablespoons maple syrup
- 4 teaspoons baking powder
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- Pinch of salt

## Method

Combine all ingredients and mix. Cook for a couple of minutes each side.
